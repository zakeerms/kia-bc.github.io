Welcome to Kerala Islamic Association of
British Columbia

KIABC is an association that was created to support the Kerala Muslim population in the Pacific Northwest and British Columbia. It is the only association of its kind in the area and has a community of over 100 families from Kerala, India.

Established in 2018


Establish an Organization that is vibrant and follows Islamic teachings, with a warm and welcoming atmosphere, excellent services, opportunities for community involvement, and a space for ideas to grow. This association should benefit not only BC Muslims but also the wider community.

KIABC is a Non-Profit Organization


KIABC is a non-profit organization that relies on the hard work and dedication of its volunteer Board of Directors and Board of Trustees to manage its operations. The organization also has dedicated team of Program Leaders who run the various programs and services that KIABC offers.
